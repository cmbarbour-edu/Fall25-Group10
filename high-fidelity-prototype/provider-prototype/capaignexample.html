Delve into the Black Abyss
Run by: @hollow.wood.13

Short description:

A group of pirates stole the soon-to-be princess. Does the party have what it takes to save her?

Extended description:

The soon-to-be princess of an elven kingdom was recently kidnapped by a group of men claiming to be working for a pirate lord who goes by "The Dread Pirate Aered." Upon the king's request, a group of adventurers was assembled to try to save the princess. It seems that the king may be hiding something from the aventurers... Do you wish to join the party and figure out who can be trusted?
