About Nat20 Network

Nat20 Network connects Dungeon Masters and players to Dungeons & Dragons campaigns. The platform helps DMs create and manage campaigns while allowing players to discover and join public campaigns, manage character sheets, and review experiences.

What we do

We reduce the barrier to joining tabletop games by providing an easy way to find and manage D&D campaigns. Features include campaign creation, player management, reviews, and administrative tools for moderation and analytics.

Core functions

Players
  • Create and modify profiles and character sheets.
  • View and subscribe to available campaigns.
  • Write reviews for campaigns and DMs.
Dungeon Masters
  • Create and manage campaigns.
  • Approve or deny player requests to join campaigns.
  • View player reviews and ratings.
System Administrators
  • Monitor platform activity and user behavior.
  • Manage user accounts and resolve disputes.
  • Access analytics and reports on platform usage.
